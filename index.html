About me

Hey, I’m André Mourão and I’m working on bringing images from the Internet of the past to the present at Arquivo.pt (Portuguese Web Archive)

I’m also the co-creator of Revisionista.PT, uncovering post-publication edits in Portuguese news articles (with Flávio Martins) and an Associated Member at NOVA LINCS research center.

My research interests include multimedia information retrieval, computer vision and machine learning techniques.

Check out the Projects section to see some of the stuff I’ve done in the past.
